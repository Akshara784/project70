World School Chess Tournament 2021

Our team stood First in the Preliminary stage qualifying now for the next one.

Congratulations to our Sanskriti School Chess Team playing the World School Chess Tournament 2021. They won 6-1 in Round 7 beating Kolleg St. Baisen from Germany. Players who played the seven boards were Ishan Garg, Aadya Gupta, Angad Minocha, Navvye Anand, Parth Choudhary, Kabir Talwar and Gaurang Tewari, with Arunima Sethi and Raunaq Singh being the other members.

Online Yoga Competition

Students Avika Singh 6F, Suhaani Duklan 6F, Urshita Gupta 9G and Rano Bhatt 9D participated in the Online Yoga Competition “ Virtual Verve’20 _ 5th Annual Inter School Championship” conducted by The Heritage School, Rohini. Our School secured 1st position in the U-17 Category.
